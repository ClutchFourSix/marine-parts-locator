Positioning and trust

A sourcing-first maritime parts platform.

Marine Parts Locator is designed to start as an efficient sourcing desk and evolve into a true marketplace once buyer demand and supplier participation are established.

Why this model works

The maritime parts market is fragmented, technical, and frequently hard to navigate. Buyers often know what broke, but not exactly what to order. Suppliers often have inventory, but inconsistent listing formats and limited discoverability.

This project begins by solving the highest-friction problem first: locating the right part quickly. That creates immediate value, supports early monetization, and generates the deal flow needed to grow into a proper marketplace.

Start lean

Search and request flows first. No heavy backend required to validate the concept.

Monetize effort

Charge for sourcing work before building advanced marketplace automation.

Scale supply

Use supplier intake and listings to create a foundation for future subscriptions and lead-routing.

Important note

Fitment and compatibility should always be verified before purchase or installation. The platform should help narrow the search and identify likely options, but technical confirmation matters.
